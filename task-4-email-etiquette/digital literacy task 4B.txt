          DO’S AND DONT’S ON SOCIAL MEDIA:

5 DO’S ON SOCIAL MEDIA:
* Use professional profiles : Create a LinkedIn profile and try to always keep it updated for networking and connecting with others.
* Learn and follow platform policies: Follow and know about the policies and rules of each social media platform to avoid any kind of violations.
* Share positively : Post content that inspires and educates people without making anyone feel disrespected or low.
* Verify information: Share only verified information data and facts as spreading wrong information can lead to misunderstandings.
* Maintain privacy settings: Keep you personal information and location private.


5 DONT’S ON SOCIAL MEDIA:
* Don’t overshare personal life: Avoid posting your address, finances etc. 
* Don’t engage in cyberbullying: Never harass or disrespect others online
* Don’t trust everything online: Everything you see online is not safe. Don’t click on any unknown links as they might contain virus.
* Don’t use fake accounts to troll/spy: Trolling is unethical and can have legal consequences.
* Don’t accept unknown friend requests blindly: Only connect with people you know.
